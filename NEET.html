INTRODUCTION TO NEET

National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test (NEET) is entrance test conducted by National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission in reputed medical colleges of INDIA .
The reputed medical colleges in India includes AIIMS(Delhi) and other AIIMS colleges across India as well as other medical colleges in various states.
This exam contains questions from PHYSICS,CHEMISTRY,BIOLOGY the syllabus is prescribed by NTA (National Testing Agency).
