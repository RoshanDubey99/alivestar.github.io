INTRODUCTION TO JEE MAINS

JEE MAINS is an all INDIA level competetive exam held for granting admission into INDIA'S most prestigious engineering colleges.
The students who are appearing or have passed the class 12th board examinations from any recognized board or university are eligible to appear in this examination
This exam contains questions of PHYSICS,CHEMISTRY,MATHEMATICS from the prescribed syllabus by NATIONAL TESTING AGENCY (NTA)
